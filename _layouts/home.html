Netflix, founded in 1997, has revolutionized the streaming industry by evolving from a DVD rental service to a global leader in video-on-demand with over 200 million subscribers. The platform's target audience includes tech-savvy users across various age groups, primarily teenagers, college students, entrepreneurs, and working professionals. Netflix's success is driven by its customer-centric approach, utilizing personalized content recommendations, multi-device accessibility, and innovative marketing strategies. Continuous data analysis and adaptation to market needs have cemented Netflix's position as a frontrunner in the digital streaming market.
